About Us

Roar Nigeria Hub is a community that provides professional support to technology enabled startups, researchers, entrepreneurs and SME’s. Her programs are designed to develop a new generation of innovators and creators that will provide local technology based solutions with a global perspective Roar Nigeria is hosted within University of Nigeria Nsukka (UNN) and is the 1st full-fledged university embedded technology Hub in West Africa, with a student population of nearly 40,000 and 12 institutions of higher learning within an hour drive from Enugu City. The Hub is a hotbed of ideas, invention and market induced solutions created with the concept of a triple helix (An interaction between Academia, Industry and Government) and hosted in a serene environment with researchers, students, industry and government within a stone throw distance.

Our Drive

Vision

To create home-grown technology solutions with **global impact**.

Mission

To produce the next generation of **entrepreneurs** by creating an enabling environment, support systems and relevant skills to succeed with technology entrepreneurship.

What We Offer

Office Space and Facilities

We provide the teams a neat **co-working space** within the hub for a professional work environment.

Mentorship and Professional Support

We provide a support system with **quality mentors** to help the teams convert their ideas into marketable products.

Platforms for Investment

We provide investment opportunities through pitch events where the teams showcase their ideas to potential investors.

Examples: Demo day, Angel Investor Networks, Competitions (Internal/External), Hackathon Sessions, Guests’ Pitching Session, Investment conferences.

Consultancy Services

  • Corporate Research and Development Support Program (CRAD): Support to corporate organisations by providing solutions through university researchers and our start-ups. This converts laboratory stage technology into commercially viable products and services, making researchers market-focused.
  • **Roar Nigeria Technology Community:** A community of tech enthusiasts providing tech solutions to companies. We train and connect members to companies that need our solutions.

Intellectual Property Rights & Legal Matters

  • We offer services in:
    • Patent & Licensing
    • Trademarks, Copyright, and Design
    • Business Intelligence
Our Focus Areas

  • Environmental Technology

Requirements to Join the Incubation Program
